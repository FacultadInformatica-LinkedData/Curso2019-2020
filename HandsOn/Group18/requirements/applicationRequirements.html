1.Introduction

This document seeks to define the project that will be developed in order to put into practice the knowledge obtained in the subject "Semantic Web, Linked Data and Knowledge Graphs".

This version (001) of the document presents the specifications given by teachers and members of the team-work, some mockups of the interfaces will be presented too.

2.Overall Description

The submitted project is about a web application, which will present to the user proposals for events and activities to be carried out in the city of Madrid, in order to improve their experience.

Using open data about events, monuments and important sites in the city of Madrid, this app can show to the user information of what to do in Madrid, could be filter by their interest in a certain area or even the proximity that exists between them.
