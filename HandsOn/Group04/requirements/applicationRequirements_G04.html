App Description

The app will show a correlation between air state and quality and the state of traffic in the distinct areas of the city

App Requisites

  • R1: Extract useful data from the CSV files
  • R2: Model connections between mesauring stations and the district, area or neighborhood which they belong to
  • R3: Link the station-by-district data to Madrid traffic RDFs which are publicly available
  • R4: Map of the city by color codes and zones/areas
