SMART CITY : LONDON

R1: Data is from London, ranked #1 smart city

R2: Data is available as a .csv file in the following URL: https://data.london.gov.uk/dataset/number-international-visitors-london

R3: Data is under OGL, the UK Open Government Licence, which allows use of the data for nearly every purpose, including academic or commercial

R4: Data is about international visitors in London, which could be linked to other datasets about population or turism in the city.
More information and datasets about London in https://data.london.gov.uk/
