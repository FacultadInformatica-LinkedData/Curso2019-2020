Culture Race

This application will allow you to explore Madrid in a way you have never seen before.

Look up for some cultural place and Culture Race will give you the best choices around you to visit later. In adition, you can track the best tour just by clicking on a single button.

Application will be writen in Java, and Javascript, using SPARQL and open datasets from governament webs.
